My Next Vacation

By Carla Tapia

Bora Bora
Bora Bora is an island in French Polynesia in the Pacific Ocean surrounded by coral reef. I want to visit Bora Bora because I would like to go scuba diving, one of its popular tourist attractions, and visit its volcanos. Bora Bora Tourism Site
Montego Bay, Jamaica
Montego Bay is a city in Jamaica known for its white sandy beaches and clear blue water. Its dry season is between Decemeber to April, which conincides with D.C.'s coldest months, making it a perfect escape from the winter. I want to visit Montego Bay for its beaches and the infamous Margaritaville. Montego Bay, Jamaica Tourism Site
